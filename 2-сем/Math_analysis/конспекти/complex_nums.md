$$
	z = (x; y); x, y \in \mathbb R
$$

$x = \Re\,z$
: дійсна частина,

$y = \Im\,z$
: уявна

$$
	z_1 \pm z_2 = (x_1; y_1) \pm (x_2; y_2) \triangleq (x_1 \pm x_2; y_1 \pm y_2)
$$
$$
	z_1 \cdot z_2 = (x_1; y_1) \cdot (x_2; y_2) \triangleq (x_1x_2 - y_1y_2; x_1y_2 + y_1x_2 )
$$
